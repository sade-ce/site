*Total Points: This is the total accumulated points you earn every paid class for a certain cutoff.
*Teacher Share Points: Teacher share is {{payment_computation?.teacher_share}}% of the total accumulated points.
*Paypal Charges: When the student buy points, paypal deduct a total of {{ payment_computation?.transaction_fee_paypal }}% from the total amount they pay. But we still give the full amount of points to student, that's why we need to deduct the {{ payment_computation?.transaction_fee_paypal }}% to the teacher, branch and company share. Company, branch and teacher will share with this deduction. Since teacher share is {{payment_computation?.teacher_share}}% then we deduct {{payment_computation?.transaction_fee_teacher_share}}% to teacher points. And the remaining will be deducted to the company and branch.
*Conversion Rate Fee: Paypal charge this fee when we transfer the money from paypal to local banks.
*Total Earnings: This amount sum of all the share and deductions.
*Average Salary: This amount is may change depends on the current exchange rate.
*Note: Additional charges will be applied to GCash and Western Union base on the total salary earned.
*Note: For paypal users, Conversion rate is not applied but paypal will charge a service fee upon receiving your salary.
