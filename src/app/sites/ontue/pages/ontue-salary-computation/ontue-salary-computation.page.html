*Total Points: This is the total accumulated points you earn every paid class for a certain cutoff.
*Teacher Share Points: Teacher share is {{payment_computation?.teacher_share}}% of the total accumulated points.
*Branch and Office Share: Branch and Office get {{100 - payment_computation?.teacher_share}}% share of the total accumulated points.
*Why branch and Company have share? Simply because without student, teacher wont be able to teach. The main purpose of branches is to get more student.
And as a company we need to pay for our servers to host website, advertisement, salaries, transactions and taxes.
*Important Note: (All share's are subject to change without prior notice due to currency fluctuation)
*Transaction Fee: When the student buy points, paypal deduct a total of 4.7% from the total amount they pay. But we still give the full amount of points to student, that's why we need to deduct the 4.7% to the teacher, branch and company share. Company, branch and teacher will share with this deduction. Since teacher share is {{payment_computation?.teacher_share}}% then we deduct {{payment_computation?.transaction_fee_teacher_share}}% to teacher points. And the remaining will be deducted to the company and branch.
*Points after transaction: remaining points after share and transaction is applied
*Salary in USD:( Paypal ) This amount is may change depends on the current exchange rate.
*Salary in PHP:( GCash and Western Union) This amount is may change depends on the current exchange rate.
*Note: For paypal users, service charge will be deduct upon receiving your salary.
*Note: For GCash and Western Union, transaction rate is applied to GCash and Western Union base on their transfer fee.
