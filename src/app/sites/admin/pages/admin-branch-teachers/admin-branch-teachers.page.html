Branch Teachers

  • You can choose teachers for your site.
  • You can add many group, level, idx separating by comma.
  • All combination of including teachers are 'OR' expression.
  • So, if you put group of 'withcener' and level '3,4,5', then you will see all withcenter teachers and teachers of level 3,4,5.
  • Teacher level range is from 1 to 5.
